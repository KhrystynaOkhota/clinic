20/04/2022

We will not talk about the war you see on the news. We are living in this war. Just a few facts.

Ukraine has been fighting russian aggression since the seizure of Crimea and parts of Donbas in 2014. The confrontation with the russian federation reached a new level in February 2022, when a comprehensive attack on Ukraine began, with the main goal of the aggression being the complete seizure of Ukraine's territory and its destruction as a nation, the so-called "denazification." The aggression was full-scale, aimed not only at the Armed Forces of Ukraine, but also at the outright destruction of civilians and infrastructure.  With the beginning of a new phase of the conflict, the consequences for the civilian population have become unheard of since the end of World War II. The official figures are staggering. 97% of the targets of russian air attacks are civilians.
